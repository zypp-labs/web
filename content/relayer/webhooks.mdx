<Callout type="warn" title="Coming soon">
  Webhook delivery is on the ZRN roadmap. When available, you'll register a callback URL per API key and receive `POST` requests when jobs reach `confirmed` or `failed`.
</Callout>

## Current alternatives

**SSE streaming** — `GET /v1/transactions/:jobId/stream` pushes updates every ~1.2s until the job reaches a terminal state. See [Stream job status](/relayer/docs/api/stream-job).

**Polling** — `GET /v1/transactions/:jobId` on a schedule. Suitable for background jobs where latency isn't critical.
